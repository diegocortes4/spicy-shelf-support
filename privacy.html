Privacy Policy

Spicy Shelf

Effective July 17, 2026

The short version. Spicy Shelf collects nothing. There are no accounts, no servers, and no analytics — everything you log stays on your device. The only time anything leaves your phone is when you search for a book (the words you type go to OpenLibrary to fetch covers) and when you make a purchase, which Apple handles.

🔍What we collect

Nothing. Spicy Shelf has no user accounts, runs no servers, and does not store, log, or receive any of your library or reading information. We can’t see your entries, and we can’t see your searches.

📱Where your data lives

Your books, ratings, tropes, shelves, reading sessions, reviews, quotes, and bingo boards are stored only on your device, using Apple’s on-device SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your iPhone through Spicy Shelf.

🔎Book search — the one network request

When you search for or add a book, Spicy Shelf sends the text you type — the book title and/or author — to OpenLibrary.org to fetch cover images and book details. That request contains only your search words — no account, no name, no device identifier, and nothing that identifies you.

We do not store, log, or link these searches to any identity, because we run no servers of our own. OpenLibrary is an independent service run by the Internet Archive and handles the request under its own privacy policy. This lookup is optional — manual entry and adding a cover photo work fully offline. No other data leaves your device, except App Store purchases, which are handled by Apple.

🚧No tracking, no analytics, no ads

Spicy Shelf does not track you across apps or websites, uses no advertising or analytics services, and contains no third-party SDKs. Our privacy manifest declares “Data Not Collected.”

💳Purchases

Shelf Plus is an optional subscription handled entirely by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. Apple processes the transaction — we never receive your name, payment details, or Apple ID. See Apple’s Privacy Policy.

⚙️Your control over your data

You can export your entire library to CSV any time from Settings. Delete all data in Settings permanently removes every entry from your device, and deleting the app removes all of its data.

👶Children

Spicy Shelf is not directed at children under 13 and collects no data from anyone.

🗒️Changes

If this policy changes, we’ll update this page and the effective date above.
